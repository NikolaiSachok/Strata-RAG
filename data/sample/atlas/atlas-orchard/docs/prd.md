# Orchard Recipes — Product Requirements (PRD)

**Category:** food / recipe manager
**Theme:** orchard, garden, fruit-picking
**Mascot:** Pip, a wisecracking apple
**Has humor:** yes — punny, food-joke heavy

## Summary
Orchard Recipes is a recipe organiser themed as a fruit orchard. You "pick" recipes
into baskets, seasonal produce is highlighted, and Pip the apple narrates with a steady
stream of food puns ("This one's the *apple* of my pie!"). The look is warm and rustic:
wooden crates, leafy greens, ripe fruit.

## Functional requirements
- Save recipes into themed "baskets" (collections)
- Tag recipes by season and main ingredient
- Shopping-list generation from selected recipes
- Pip mascot delivers a contextual pun on key screens

## Theme requirements
The orchard/garden metaphor must be consistent: collections are baskets, favourites are
"ripe", archived recipes are "preserves".
