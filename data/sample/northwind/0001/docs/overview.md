# Citrus Garden — Overview

**Category:** productivity / to-do list
**Theme:** citrus fruit (lemon, lime), garden growth
**Mascot:** Zest, a pun-telling cartoon lemon
**Has humor:** yes — light, pun-driven

## What it is
A task manager themed around growing a citrus tree. Completing tasks grows fruit;
filling the tree completes your day. Designed to make routine chores feel rewarding.

## Key screens
- Today's list (the "garden bed")
- The tree (visual progress for the day)
- Weekly basket (summary of completed tasks)

## Theme notes
The fruit-and-garden metaphor runs through everything: streaks are "vines", overdue
tasks "wilt", and the weekly summary is a "harvest basket". Zest the lemon mascot is
the source of the app's humor.
