Visual / theme design requirements for Citrus Garden

Palette: warm lemon yellow, lime green, soft cream background. Rounded, friendly shapes.
Mascot: Zest, a cartoon lemon with expressive eyes; appears on empty states and on the
harvest screen. Typography should feel hand-drawn but legible at small sizes.

Mood: cheerful, low-pressure, garden-cozy. Animations are gentle (fruit gently bobbing,
vines growing in). Avoid harsh reds for overdue items — use a wilting muted green instead.
