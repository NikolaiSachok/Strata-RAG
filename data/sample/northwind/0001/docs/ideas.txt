Gameplay / concept ideas for Citrus Garden

The core loop: each completed task waters the citrus tree and grows one piece of fruit.
A full tree ends the day with a "harvest" celebration. Streaks are modeled as healthy
vines; missed days cause a vine to wilt until the next completed task revives it.

Stretch ideas:
- Seasonal themes (a winter citrus grove with frost effects).
- A weekly "basket" you can share as an image.
- Zest the lemon mascot reacts with puns when you finish a hard task.
