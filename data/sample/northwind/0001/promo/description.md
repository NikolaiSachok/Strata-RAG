# Citrus Garden — Promo Description

Citrus Garden is a cheerful to-do and chore app wrapped in a bright lemon-and-lime
theme. Every task you finish ripens a fruit on your little tree, and when the tree is
full you "harvest" your day. The whole app leans into a fresh, juicy citrus look:
sunny yellows, leafy greens, and a friendly cartoon lemon mascot named Zest who cracks
a groan-worthy pun every time you check something off ("You're really on a *roll* —
get it?").

The tone is light and a bit silly on purpose: chores are boring, so the jokes and the
growing fruit are there to make a dull list feel like tending a tiny garden. Core
features: daily and recurring tasks, a streak counter shaped like a vine, and a weekly
"basket" summary of everything you harvested.
