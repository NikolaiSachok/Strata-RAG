# Orbit Focus — Promo Description

Orbit Focus is a calm, minimalist focus timer set in deep space. You pick a task, start
a session, and a small spacecraft drifts along its orbit while you work; finishing a
focus block lets it dock at a station and "refuel". The visual theme is astronomy and
the night sky: dark navy backgrounds, soft starfields, slow planetary motion. There is
no mascot and no jokes — the design is deliberately serious and quiet, meant to reduce
distraction rather than entertain.

Core features: a configurable Pomodoro-style timer, an orbit map that fills in as you
complete sessions, and a constellation view summarising your focused hours over a week.
