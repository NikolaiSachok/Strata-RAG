# Tide Tracker — Overview

**Category:** health / habit tracker
**Theme:** ocean, tides, marine life
**Mascot:** Marina, a friendly cartoon sea turtle
**Has humor:** yes — gentle, sea-pun flavoured

## What it is
A habit-tracking app themed around the ocean. Each habit is a "current"; keeping a
streak raises the tide, and Marina the sea turtle surfaces to celebrate milestones with
a mild sea pun ("You're on a *roll*-ing wave!"). Missed habits make the tide go out.

## Key screens
- Habits (the "shoreline")
- Tide chart (streak visualisation)
- Reef (long-term progress, where completed habits become coral)

## Theme notes
Marine throughout: tides, currents, reefs, coral. Humor is present but gentle, carried
by Marina the turtle.
