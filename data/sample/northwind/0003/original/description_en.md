# Tide Tracker — Original Store Description (EN)

Build better habits with the calm of the sea. Tide Tracker turns each habit into an
ocean current: keep your streak and watch the tide rise; let it slip and the water
recedes. Marina, our friendly sea turtle, pops up to cheer your milestones (and to
share the occasional gentle sea pun).

- Track daily and weekly habits as ocean "currents"
- Watch your reef grow as habits become long-term routines
- A weekly tide chart shows your consistency at a glance

Calm, encouraging, and just a little playful.
